HE IS ABLE EVANGELICAL MISSION

HE-IS-ABLE EVANGELICAL MISSION started as a little fellowship, committed to the studying of God’s

Word, evangelism and aggressive prayers, with six brethren in 1993, in Emeabiam, Owerri West local

government, Imo state, Nigeria. It started Sunday service in February 1994, in the same Emeabiam,

Owerri West local government area, Imo state. Between then and now, the church has grown and

still growing with branches in many states in Nigeria, west, central and south Africa. The focus of the

church has been in the following areas:

1. Soul winning

2. Discipleship of God’s people based on the wholesome Word of God

3. Preparing for the glorious rapture of the saints

4. Bringing down God’s awesome power for great revival in believers’ life, with the fire spreading
among them that are not yet saved.

The Lord has been faithful unto the church until now.
